About Us

Out & Proud Apparel was founded in 2021 with a mission to create high-quality, fashionable clothing that celebrates the LGBTQ+ community. We believe that clothing is a powerful way to express yourself and show your pride, and we are proud to offer a wide range of styles and sizes for everyone. Our designs are inspired by the diverse and vibrant community we serve, and we are committed to using sustainable and ethical practices in all aspects of our business.
